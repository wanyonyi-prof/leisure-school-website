About Our School

Leisure Rondwe Jalaram Girls Secondary School is a premier institution committed to providing transformative education and nurturing future female leaders. Our core values — honesty, hard work, discipline, and compassion — drive everything we do.

We create a supportive environment where students grow intellectually, socially, and morally, becoming responsible citizens. Our modern teaching methods, dedicated faculty, and strong mentorship ensure every girl reaches her full potential.

Since our founding, we have maintained a legacy of academic excellence and holistic development, preparing students not only for national examinations but also for life beyond the classroom.
